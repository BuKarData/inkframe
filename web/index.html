Features

Everything you need, beautifully displayed

Always-On Display

E-ink technology means your display is always visible without using power. Gentle on eyes, perfect for any lighting.

Privacy First

Your data stays yours. All processing happens locally on your device. No tracking, no analytics.

Modular Design

Choose what matters to you. Weather, calendar, tasks, or photos. Customize your dashboard your way.

Ultra Low Power

Weeks of battery life on a single charge. E-ink only uses power when updating the screen.

Easy Setup

Connect to WiFi, link your account, and you are ready. No complex configuration needed.

Open Platform

Built on ESP32 with open firmware. Extend, modify, and make it truly yours.
